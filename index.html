About Me

I'm Samantha, previously a Master stylist and educator. You can rarely catch me at home, as I'm probably visiting any city or that I get the chance to. One of the best ways to grow, in my opinion, is to see the world from new perspectives. It gives you the chance to apply that practice to other situations.

Coming from the world of beauty is a paralelled and at the same time oddly similar environment to the coding world. There was nothing like feeling that I crushed a new technique. In the world of code there are so many different languages to learn. Unlike hair, most of what I need is my computer, Google, and practice. in hair I need someones private time, new tools, travel, and something to practice on. The best similarity between the hair and programming worlds has to be the ability to create.
